Dataset Overview¶

PISA2012 dataset is a very rich dataset with 636 columns and 4885490 rows. PISA 2012 is a survey of students' skills and knowledge as they approach the end of compulsory education. This survey is about how well-prepared they are for life beyond school.

(Distribution of Scores)¶

The three histograms show the normal distribution of scores in Math, Reading, and Science. It can be seen, the red line shows the median of scores which is less than 500 points.

(Relationship between Scores and Gender)¶

In this part, the box plots show the relationship between scores in Math, Reading, and Science with genders. All scores are nearly the same except the reading score is highest for females.

(Correlation between scores, genders, and parents' education levels)¶

The three box plots illustrate the correlation between subjects' scores, genders, and parents' education levels. It can be seen, the scores of all genders are nearly the same but students who are parents have master's or doctoral certificates have high scores.
